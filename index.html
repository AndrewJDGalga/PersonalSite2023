In The Dark companion timer

Companion web app for prototype board game currently named "In the Dark" and under developement by a local company. The app is a semi-random timer intended to provide unease to players of the board game. Once the timer is started, players are treated to scary audio and the knowledge they have about 4 minutes before their time runs out!

The front end uses a simple HTML and Sass/SCSS structure with the app itself coded in Javascript. A very simple backend was requested and coded in PHP to allow for modification to the audio files used and timer and animation timings.

ATX Game Designers

Website completed for the Austin, Texas based board game design group ATX Game Designers for the final Practicum portion of my Austin Community College Web Development coursework. ATX Game Designers asked me to make a static site in Wordpress on a server they control. I had almost no experience with PHP or Wordpress at that point. I created a Classic-style theme and added site content directly in Wordpress.

In order to complete my course, I was later asked to rebuild the home and game gallery pages from scratch using HTML, Vanilla JS, and CSS. I completed the rebuild of these pages, including the creation of a custom image carousel, in 10 days.
